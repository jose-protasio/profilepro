About me

Hello, my name is José and I help people succeed with their projects by applying my programming skills and knowledge. As someone who loves coding, I see that a clean and performant code is of paramount importance for the success of a system, so I will always put into practice all the knowledge I gained in my career as a Software Developer. With the beginning of the pandemic, the reality of Caldas Novas (Go) changed drastically, as being a tourist city, the city's economy depends exclusively on people, however, the borders were closed to control Covid-19, which left Caldas Novas scarce jobs. As a son and student, I always had the dream of conquering my own things and helping my family. It was then that I made the most difficult and important decision of my life: to go to Blumenau-Sc in search of opportunities in the technology field. So I fell in love with programming, because I've always been very curious to know how things work behind the scenes. During this time living here, I worked in two technology companies, with Html, CSS, Javascript, ReactJs and NodeJs, acting as Junior Front-End Developer.
